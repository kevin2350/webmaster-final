Model Design




Safety comes first...

Each of our models are designed with safety in mind. Specialized front and side impact protection minimizies deceleration and aftershock in the event of a crash. The frame of our models are designed to absorb as much energy as possible, helping to reduce occupant velocity in crashes. In order to reduce rollover risk, the center of weight for our models are all positioned very low to help hold the car down firmly.



Extended warranty...

Our models each come with a 4 year or 60,000 mile warranty. Regardless of which one you reach first, we have you covered. In addition to the car itself, our warranty covers problems with the engine, transmission/transaxle, transfer case, and drivetrain. NRust and corrosion with also be covered. All OHM vehicles are also provided with a Federal Emissions warranty, lasting 8 years and covering you if your vehicle doesn't meet the EPA-required emissions standards.



Peak performance...

While affordable, our vehicles are also designed for top-notch performance. OHM's All-Wheel Drive runs on two motors, each helping to control wheel torque and reduce skidding. Each vehicle is capable of running on either motor, so if one breaks down you're car will still operate efficiently. Hyper responsive wheels and brakes, combined with lowered suspension, gives the driver secure control over the vehicle at all times.

Why Go Electric?

With electric vehicles rapidly rising in popularity... What makes them better?


Electric vehicles cost less to operate than normal vehicles - the electricty required to charge your car costs significantly less than using petroleum.


All you need to charge an electric vehicle is an electric grid. As such, your car can be charged virtually anywhere where you have access to electricity. The U.S, along with many other countries, are rapidly establishing charging stations as well.


You're helping our world. Electric vehicles produce much less pollution than gas-dependent cars. As an environmentally concerned company, OHM vehicles are also made with eco-friendly materials.
